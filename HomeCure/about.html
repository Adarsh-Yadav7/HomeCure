About Homecure

Homecure is your all-in-one health companion, offering AI-powered health predictions, medical guidance, and emergency assistance. Our mission is to empower individuals with accessible health insights and reliable first-aid support.

Our Features

  • 💡 **Health Predictions:** Check for Diabetes, Obesity, and Baby Gender with AI-based models.
  • 📚 **Health Library:** Explore articles and exercises for a healthier life.
  • 🩺 **AI Chatbot:** Get instant answers to your health-related queries.
  • 🚑 **Emergency Help:** Access first-aid guides and helpline numbers.

Why Choose Homecure?

We believe in **prevention, awareness, and immediate response** to health concerns. With AI-driven insights and practical remedies, Homecure helps you stay informed and prepared.
